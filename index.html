How Genie Workforce Temporary & Event Job Process in Pune

At Genie Workforce, we directly employ individuals for temporary jobs... At Genie Workforce, we directly employ individuals for temporary jobs in Pune, including short-term and event-based assignments. Our process is designed to ensure transparency, punctuality, and smooth coordination for both the Genies and the clients they assist.
1. Finding and Applying for Work All event helper and part time job opportunities in Pune are listed on our website and app, including venue, date, time, and dress code details. Genies can browse through these listings and apply for the tasks that best match their preferences and availability.
2. Selection Process After showing interest, shortlisted Genies receive a verification call from the GenieWorkforce team. The selection is based on profile completion, skills, reliability, and work history. Only qualified applicants are confirmed for available roles as event staff in Pune.
3. Joining the WhatsApp Group Selected Genies are added to a dedicated WhatsApp group for updates and real-time coordination before and during the event.
4. Task Preparation & Check-in Genies must check in via the app 1 hour before the task begins and reach the venue 30 minutes early. On arrival, they must contact the event coordinator through the WhatsApp group for instructions.
5. During the Task Genies are expected to maintain discipline, follow event guidelines, and perform their assigned responsibilities responsibly. Professionalism during these temporary jobs in Pune can help increase eligibility for future assignments.
6. Check-out & Payment After completing the task, Genies check out via the app. If attendance and check-ins are logged correctly, payment is processed within 7 days through Cashfree redeemable via bank or UPI.
7. Experience Certificate & Profile Sharing Within 10 days of task completion, Genies receive an experience certificate that highlights their event contributions. A public profile showcasing completed work, reviews, and ratings is also available, serving as a verified resume for future event opportunities.
